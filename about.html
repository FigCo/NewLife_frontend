New Life's Values

Because Jesus is the head of this church we expect every leader and stave to uphold these six core values.

We seek to demonstrate a spirit of cooperation not competition in our language and attitudes.

We work at maintaining excellence and quality in all that we do since we are a reflection of God.

We seek to treat all people with respect and dignity regardless of their position or social status.

We value a faith-filled positive outlook that finds solutions and takes initiative to overcome challenges.

We value integrity of character that allows us to accomplish our mission without compromising our testimony.

We value a close walk with Jesus Christ through the Holy Spirit that keeps our soul in line with the heart of God.

  • GOD

    God is the Creator and Ruler of the universe. He has eternally existed in three persons: the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. These three are co-equal and are one God.

    Genesis 1:1,26,27; 3:22; Psalm 90:2; Matthew 28:19; 1 Peter 1:2; 1 Corinthians 13:14

  • Eternity

    We all were created to exist forever. We will either exist eternally separated from God by sin, or in union with God through forgiveness and salvation. The place of eternal separation from God is called Hell. Heaven is the place of eternal union and eternal life with God.

    John 3:16; Romans 6:23; Revelation 20:15 (hell); Matthew 25:41; Revelation 21:27 (heaven)

  • Salvation

    Salvation is a gift from God to mankind. We can never make up for our sin by self-improvement or good works. Only by trusting in Jesus Christ as God's offer of forgiveness can we be saved from sin's penalty. Eternal life begins the moment we receive Jesus Christ into our life by repentance and faith.

    Romans 6:23; Ephesians 2:8,9; John 14:6; John 1:12; Titus 3:5; Galatians 3:26; Romans 5:1

  • Mankind

    We all were created to exist forever. We will either exist eternally separated from God by sin, or in union with God through forgiveness and salvation. The place of eternal separation from God is called Hell. Heaven is the place of eternal union and eternal life with God.

    John 3:16; Romans 6:23; Revelation 20:15 (hell); Matthew 25:41; Revelation 21:27 (heaven)

  • Jesus Christ

    Jesus Christ is the Son of God. He is co-equal with the Father. Jesus lived a sinless human life and offered Himself as the perfect sacrifice for the sins of all by dying on a cross. He arose from the dead after three days to demonstrate His power over sin and death. He ascended to Heaven's glory and will return again to earth to reign as King of Kings, and Lord of Lords.

    Matthew 1:22,23; Isaiah 9:6; John 1:1-5; John 14:10-30; Hebrews 4:14,15; 1 Corinthians 15:3,4; Romans 1:3,4; Acts 1:9-11; 1 Timothy 6:14,15; Titus 2:13

Pastor Mark & Dee Jobe Lead Pastor

Mark Jobe is the Senior Pastor of New Life Community Church. He and his wife Dee have seen New Life grow from a handful of people to thousands meeting at 19 campuses with 33 worship services each weekend. Pastor Mark is the founder of New Life Centers, a non-profit organization providing critical support systems for youth in under-served areas of Chicago (www.nlcenters.org). He has been featured in numerous publications and books including "A Heart for the City", "Cultural Change & Your Church", "The Multi-Site Revolution" and "Amazing Faith". Pastor Mark can be heard on his daily radio program, "Straighttalk". Recorded broadcasts can be heard at www.pastormarkjobe.com

Mark holds a BA from Columbia International University, an MA from Moody Theological Seminary and a Doctorate from Bakke Graduate University.
